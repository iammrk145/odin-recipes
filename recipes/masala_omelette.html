Masala omelette

Description

A spicy Indian twist on the classic omelette — perfect for breakfast.

ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this masala omelette recipe:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 small onion(chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • Salt, turmeric, chili powder
  • Fresh coriander

Steps:

  1. Beat eggs with spices and chopped veggies.

  2. Heat oil in a pan.

  3. Pour mixture and cook both sides. Done!
